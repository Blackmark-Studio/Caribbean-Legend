Sailing
{
The ability to helm a vessel in any conditions, use instruments and command a squadron. Even the most capricious and unwieldy ship will obey the steady hand of an experienced skipper.
 Depends on 'Perception' and 'Talent' (I0.2 + T0.8)
 ~
 To command a vessel of a given rate a corresponding minimum Navigation level is required:
 rate 1 requires 90
 rate 2 requires 70
 rate 3 requires 55
 rate 4 requires 40
 rate 5 requires 20
 rate 6 requires 01
 ~
 A lack of Navigation leads to penalties to all the hero's skills and attributes. The greater the difference between the current and required Navigation value, the stronger the penalties.

Personal_abilities
{
Perks that expand the character’s personal abilities. You gain progress by leveling up personal skills.
 ~
 Upon reaching a threshold, you gain a new personal Perk point. Different Perks require a different number of points. Usually, the most powerful ones are at the end of the development tree.
}
Ship_abilities
{
Perks that affect ship management. You gain progress by leveling up ship skills.
 ~
 Upon reaching a threshold, you gain a new ship Perk point. Different Perks require a different number of points. Usually, the most powerful ones are at the end of the development tree.
 ~
 They can be inherited by the main character from officers assigned to ship positions who know their craft.
}

Rank is an indicator of a character’s level of development in the game. You gain progress by leveling up skills.
 ~
 As the character’s Rank increases, their Health and Energy grow, and stronger enemies and new items begin to appear more often.

Life
{
HP — a measure of a character's resistance to physical damage. Zero means death, one means a world of possibilities.
 ~
 Value depends on P and E and grows with rank.
