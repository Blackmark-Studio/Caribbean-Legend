{
This is an interactive narrative where your actions and preparation affect a small story.
First, you are faced with a situation—this text is already shown on the screen.
Based on the situation you get, you can take certain actions.

Actions in Stories are divided into Reactions and Decisions.
Each Decision or Reaction may include a check of the character's skills, perks, or attributes.
Some actions may be unavailable if you do not meet certain requirements.
~
Any check in a Story takes many factors affecting success into account. They are listed in the tooltip on hover.
Check results do not change after reloading, only when the conditions change. Do not be discouraged by failure—next time, luck will surely be on your side.
